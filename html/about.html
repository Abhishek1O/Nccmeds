About NccMeds

Welcome to NCC Meds, your one-stop-shop for all your healthcare needs. We are a team of dedicated professionals passionate about providing high-quality health services to everyone, with a special focus on Nepal Commerce Campus (NCC) students and staffs. Our team consist of Abhishek Bhandari and Alok Bikram Khadka, students of Nepal Commerce Campus, who share a vision of making healthcare affordable and accessible for all. As a company, we offer a range of services, including online consultations, prescription delivery, and health assistants. At NCC Meds, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, which is why we offer discounts to NCC students and staff. We understand that being a student or staff member can be stressful, which is why we want to make sure that your healthcare needs are taken care of. We are committed to providing the best possible healthcare services and products to our customers. Our team of experts is always available to answer any questions or concerns you may have. We are dedicated to your health and well-being and are here to assist you every step of the way. Thank you for choosing NCC Meds, and we look forward to serving you.
